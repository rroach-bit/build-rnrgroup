09 — Usage Guidelines

Brand Do's & Don'ts

Consistency builds trust. These guidelines protect the brand's integrity across all applications.

✓ Do

  • Use the full-color logo on white or light gray backgrounds
  • Maintain minimum clear space around the logo at all times
  • Use the navy-to-teal gradient for hero sections and feature cards
  • Pair Outfit headings with DM Sans body text consistently
  • Apply the blueprint grid pattern at low opacity (3–6%) as texture
  • Use teal for interactive elements, CTAs, and links
  • Keep photography sharp, well-lit, and architecturally focused

✗ Don't

  • Place the logo on busy photographic backgrounds without a solid overlay
  • Rotate, skew, distort, or add drop shadows to the logo
  • Use unapproved colors or swap navy and teal roles
  • Set body text in Outfit or headlines in Space Mono
  • Use the patterns at high opacity — they should be subtle texture, not decoration
  • Mix the brand palette with other blues or greens not in the system
  • Use low-resolution or pixelated versions of the logo
